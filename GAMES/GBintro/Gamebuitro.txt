Gamebuitro
A Gamebuino Meta intro released at Outline 2024

"It ain't much, but it's honest work."

My most advanced code ever and my first serious experiment with C++ ;)
All (poorly) coded from scratch except for the music player.


Code & GFX: Exocet
Music: Chavez & Josss
Music player: PMF Player by JarkkoPFC, adapted by r043v/dph

Many thanks to Chavez and Josss for making such a great tune for this little intro.


Gamebuino Meta is a Arduino Zero-based handheld console:
- Microchip ATSAMD21 ARM Cortex M0+ CPU
- 32KB RAM
- 80x64 resolution
